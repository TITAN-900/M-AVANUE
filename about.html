ABOUT US

M AVANUE 是一家专注于中型卡车与重型车辆零件供应的专业公司， 主要提供 U-Bolt、车轮螺丝、悬挂系统零件以及重卡弹簧相关产品。 我们致力于为马来西亚市场提供高品质、耐用且可靠的重卡零件解决方案， 广泛服务于维修厂、经销商及商业运输行业客户。 凭借稳定的产品品质、专业的服务态度以及长期合作理念， M AVANUE 持续为客户提供值得信赖的重型车辆零件支持。

M AVANUE is a professional supplier specializing in medium-duty and heavy-duty truck spare parts, with strong focus on U-Bolts, wheel bolts, suspension components, and heavy vehicle spring systems. We are committed to delivering durable, reliable, and high-quality heavy-duty truck solutions for workshops, distributors, and commercial transport industries across Malaysia. With consistent product quality, professional service, and long-term business support, M AVANUE continues to provide trusted heavy-duty spare part solutions for our customers.
